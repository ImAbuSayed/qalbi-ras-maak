# Raw Content: قلبي رقص معاك (Qalbi Ra’s Ma’ak) – My Heart Danced With You


<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-song">Song</h2>



<p>قلبي رقص معاك (Qalbi Ra’s Ma’ak) – My Heart Danced With You</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-lyrics">Lyrics</h2>



<p>&#8216;Einy la&#8217;et &#8216;einak wel sahra bada&#8217;et<br>(My eye found yours and the evening began)</p>



<p>El donia we&#8217;fet we &#8216;albi geh yor&#8217;os<br>(The world stopped and my heart came to dance)</p>



<p>Eidek fi eidi wel naharda helwa<br>(Your hand in my hand, and today is beautiful)</p>



<p>Ehsas gedid mesh &#8216;aref ashraho<br>(A new feeling I don&#8217;t know how to explain)</p>



<p>&#8216;Albi ra&#8217;as ma&#8217;ak we ghanna leek<br>(My heart danced with you and sang for you)</p>



<p>Kol khatwa betzidni ginoon feek<br>(Every step makes me crazier about you)</p>



<p>Ya habibi, khaleeni aghanni leek<br>(Oh my love, let me sing for you)</p>



<p>Mesh hansa el yom da, da yomna el &#8216;azeem<br>(I won&#8217;t forget this day, this is our great day)</p>



<p>El leil hady, bass &#8216;albi sakheb<br>(The night is calm, but my heart is loud)</p>



<p>Kalamak sehr, we basmetak takhodni<br>(Your words are magic, and your smile captivates me)</p>



<p>Wa&#8217;edni dayman tefdal &#8216;orayeb<br>(Promise me you&#8217;ll always stay close)</p>



<p>Mahma el bo&#8217;d yizeed, ana mesh haseeb<br>(No matter how the distance grows, I won&#8217;t leave)</p>



<p>Ya negoom, &#8216;ooloo lo enni hena<br>(Oh stars, tell him that I&#8217;m here)</p>



<p>Wel &#8216;amar shahed, wel bahr ghanna<br>(And the moon is a witness, and the sea sang)</p>



<p>Law fi gharam ahla men da, zamano<br>(If there is a love sweeter than this, it&#8217;s impossible)</p>



<p>Mesh ha&#8217;dar asada&#8217;, enti ahlami<br>(I can&#8217;t believe it, you are my dreams)</p>



<p>&#8216;Albi ra&#8217;as ma&#8217;ak, we ghanna leek<br>(My heart danced with you, and sang for you)</p>



<p>Mesh hansa el yom da, da yomna el &#8216;azeem<br>(I won&#8217;t forget this day, this is our great day)</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-arabic-lyrics-with-translation">Arabic Lyrics with Translation</h2>



<p><strong>الليـــالي (Verse 1)</strong><br>عيني لقت عينك والسهرة بدأت<br>الدنيا وقفت وقلبي جه يرقص<br>إيدك في إيدي والنهارده حلوة<br>إحساس جديد مِش عارف أشرحه<br>(Translation: My eyes met yours, and the night began / The world paused, and my heart started to dance / Your hand in mine, tonight is sweet / A new feeling I can’t explain.)</p>



<p><strong>الكـــورس (Chorus)</strong><br>قلبي رقص معاك وغنّى ليك<br>كل خطوة بتزيدني جنون فيك<br>يا حبيبي، خليني أغني ليك<br>مش هنسى اليوم ده، ده يومنا العظيم<br>(Translation: My heart danced with you and sang for you / Every step makes me crazier for you / My love, let me sing to you / I’ll never forget today—it’s our grand day.)</p>



<p><strong>اللـــيل (Verse 2)</strong><br>الليل هادي، بس قلبي صاخب<br>كلامك سحر، وبسمتك تاخدني<br>وعدني دايما تفضل قريب<br>مهما البعد يزيد، أنا مش هسيب<br>(Translation: The night is calm, but my heart is loud / Your words are magic, your smile takes me / Promise me you’ll always stay close / No matter the distance, I won’t let go.)</p>



<p><strong>الجـــسر (Bridge)</strong><br>يا نجوم، قولوا له إني هنا<br>والقمر شاهد، والبحر غنّى<br>لو في غرام أحلى من ده، زمانه<br>مش هقدر أصدق، إنتِ أحلامي<br>(Translation: Oh stars, tell him I’m here / The moon witnessed, the sea sang / If love sweeter than this exists, where is it? / I can’t believe it—you’re my dreams.)</p>



<p><strong>الخاتمـــة (Outro)</strong><br>قلبي رقص معاك، وغنّى ليك<br>مش هنسى اليوم ده، ده يومنا العظيم<br>(Translation: My heart danced with you and sang for you / I’ll never forget today—it’s our grand day.)</p>
